Quality: About This Data Set

This data is about Wenyan's daily activities and locations visited from 12 Oct to 17 Oct, collected by AWARE mobile app, using various sensors in mobile.

Is the data complete?

The data may not not complete, the location plug in was not correctly set up and the records of location were missing from 12 Oct to 15 Oct. On top of that, the accuracy of mobile data is questionable, a large portion of my activities records have no or "unknown" activities, and some timestamps of my location records were missing.

Is the data coherent?

The location data matches my expectation, (most of records were from my home and CMU), most valid activity records are "stationary" or "walking", this also matches my daily activity pattern.

Is the data correct?

I think most of the records is correct as both locations and activities matches my daily schedule, but I tend to leave my phone in locker most of time when I visit Gym or workout, that result in some incoherency to my daily activities.
